1. Identify Usability Problems

Description

The Zaslavsky Lab of Brown University

The reason why I choose this website is

  1. As a website that introduces the lab to the general public, the design of the website appears confusing and uncomfortable in many ways.
  1. The layout of the website is confusing and the presentation of information is ambiguous
  1. Lack of many components like navigation, button, consideration of usability and memorability, etc.
  1. Disaster color theme and navigation bar.

Problems

Usability

Problem

  1. Conflict on real navigation and Brownâ€™s default navigation.
  1. No consistency and standards on different section of contents.
  1. Almost no interaction components, full of inappropriately arranged words.
  1. No aesthetic design

Solution

  1. Develop independent navigation
  1. Redesigned the layout of web page, rearranged different sections of contents appropriately.
  1. Add more interact components and redesigned the display of words.
  1. Designed interaction, components, words, color theme, responsive effect, etc.

Learnability & Memorability

Problem

  1. Low affordance on shallow navigation and dummy buttons
  1. Users cannot quickly find useful information about the Lab because of the bad hierarchy
  1. No system feedback of user action and status, low memorability
  1. No visual clues

Solution

  1. Redesigned navigation and buttons
  1. Special design for highlighting the bullet information
  1. Created many interact components for users with different hover effect.
  1. More intuitive visual clues.

Accessibility

There exists some low contrast errors about the footer of this page. Generally, since there is actually No Design in this page, it is meaningless to talk about accessibility itself.
